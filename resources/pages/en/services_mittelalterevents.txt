<p>Mittelalter Events is a german platform where you can get information on upcoming medieval markets and other related events. 
It's not just a calendar, rather it is a community focused platform where you can comment, follow and participate on events as well
as chat with other members. This way you can not only view upcoming events, but also get to know other people from the medieval market
scene and make new friends.</p>
