Casual Game Engine is a 2D singleplayer top-down shooter engine which you can use to create your own 2D top-down shooter games.<br/><br/>
Therefore your game behavior and entities are implemented via AngelScript. You can define a main game and also mods of your game.<br/><br/>
The engine supports connection to Steam, however this is not a requirement. When connecting with Steam you can utilize the Workshop and
Steam Achievement feature.<br/><br/>
