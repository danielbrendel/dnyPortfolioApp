AC-Resources is a hosting service for all kinds of community made resources for AssaultCube.
<br/><br/>

There you can download and share custom maps, skins, textures, sprites, models, scripts, mods & more for AssaultCube.
<br/><br/>

This is just my way to give back something to the AssaultCube community, because I really like the game very much.
Not only does it have very good gameplay mechanics and feel, it does also provide vast possibilities of modding.
<br/><br/>

You can create own maps, models, skins, sprites, textures and scripts. You can also create your own mods. There has been
evolved a big community around AssaultCube. And it does also provide dedicated servers.
