Danigram is a platform system for sharing images among the community.<br/><br/>
Users can share images using hashtags. Images are shown in a feed which can be
sorted by latest posts or top posts. Users have a personal profile where their
posts are shown. You can also share stories which are deleted by a specific
amount of time. Users can connect to each other by sending direct messages.
Also users receive notifications for certain events<br/>
<br/>
