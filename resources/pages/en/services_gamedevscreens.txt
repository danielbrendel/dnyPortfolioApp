<p>gamedevscreens.com is home of the <a href="https://www.reddit.com/r/gamedevscreens/">r/gamedevscreens</a> Twitter bot. 
It posts hot content from that subreddit to Twitter a few times per day. It is capable of posting images, GIFs and 
small sized videos. It is useful if you are interested in media from other indie game developers and have them right into 
your Twitter feed. </p>
