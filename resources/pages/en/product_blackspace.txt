Black Space is a 2D singleplayer top-down space shooter game as arena style.<br/><br/>
It is meant for quick time wasting. It is fast paced and a match is intented to run short means
this game is especially suited for some small wait time or when you are bored. Just quickly launch
the game, open a match and shoot up your opponents.<br/><br/>
The game supports dynamic game modes implementing them in AngelScript.<br/><br/>
