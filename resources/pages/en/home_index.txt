Hey there! Welcome to my website. I am an indie software developer and founder of several projects.<br/><br/>

Software development, especially programming, is a way to combine creativity, logic and art. It is my way of
expressing myself and create what I am passionate about and what I find enjoyable.<br/><br/>

OpenSource is the best way of sharing software projects. Not only ensures it that software is free
and reachable for everyone, it does also provide great community opportunities. People can learn from
the software and provide feedback to the creator. OpenSource software is used everywhere and does still
maintain a high security safety. Many of my projects are open-source because I think free software is
the best way to share knowledge.<br/><br/>
