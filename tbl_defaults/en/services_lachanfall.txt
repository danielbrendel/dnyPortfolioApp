Lachanfall.co is a german platform where user-generated content in form of funny images, jokes, memes, gifs
and videos are provided.<br/><br/>
Users can lookup the latest content, the best rated content or the content of the users they are 
following.<br/><br/>
The platform is meant to be a social network, which grants users to interact with each others in various ways.
For instance you can send private messages, there is a profile and geo search, as well as a forum for 
exchange.<br/><br/>
The content is categorised via hashtags, so users have a significant impact on how the content is made 
available.<br/><br/>
Users are informed about specific events via various notifications. There are also much more functions 
available.<br/><br/>
