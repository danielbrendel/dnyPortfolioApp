HelpRealm is a lightweight SaaS support ticket system suited for freelancers and small teams.<br/><br/>
It comes without almost any paid plans and even without ads. It is made with love and provided with love<br/><br/>
It works in the way that you get a personal support contact form which you send to your customers, by
e.g. linking to it on your website or embedding it in an iframe. Your customers can then use that form
to create support requests. If you don't want to use the predefined form you can also create your own
and use the API (you need to buy access) to create tickets.<br/><br/>
I am using HelpRealm myself for certain products, but also wanted others to be able to use it.<br/><br/>
If you are a blogger, influencer, freelancer and have an own website or other channel you can use
HelpRealm to manage your customer support requests.<br/><br/>
It is very easy and intuitive to use. Also you can manage your agents if you work together with a team.<br/><br/>
