GamingPals.org is an international platform where gamers can find other gaming buddies.<br/><br/>
Therfore they can create adverts to specify certain criteria that possible interests shall met.
You can specify a title, a description, the game and the country. Also you can limit the
amount of possible buddies and also restrict the advert to a specific gender only.<br/><br/>
Other gamers can mark themselves as interested or set them as an actual participant. There is a thread-like
messaging system where users can talk to each other in an advert. Also you can specify tags to refine an advert
which users can search for via the advert feed.<br/><br/>
At the startpage you have a feed which lists all active adverts. You can filter the results by game, location,
date and tags.<br/><br/>
You can also send private messages. Notifications are served on various events allowing you to stay updated.<br/><br/>
